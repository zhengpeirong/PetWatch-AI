Pet owners often notice health problems too late.

Small changes can be easy to miss during busy daily routines, especially when a pet still looks mostly normal.

  • ✓Pets cannot clearly explain pain, discomfort, fatigue, or breathing difficulty.
  • ✓Early symptoms can be subtle: limping, lower activity, abnormal breathing, repetitive licking, or posture changes.

  • ✓Daily short videos can create a simple personal health baseline for each pet.

Subtle symptoms

No daily baseline

What can be seen from video?

PetWatch flags visible risk signals. It does not claim to identify diseases or replace professional evaluation.

Gait and limping

Looks for asymmetry, reduced weight on one leg, slower walking, or hesitation during movement.

Posture and pain signals

Flags visible tension, crouching, guarding, stiffness, or unusual resting positions.

Resting breathing

Observes visible breathing pace and effort when the pet appears to be resting.

Activity changes

Compares movement, play, and responsiveness against a pet's normal daily baseline.

Repetitive licking

Highlights frequent paw licking, scratching, or repeated attention to one area of the body.
